Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All your journal entries and data are stored securely in your browser's local storage. We do not collect or share your data with third parties.

Do I need an API key to use the app?

No. The app comes with a local AI fallback that works offline. You can optionally connect your own API key for advanced AI models (Groq, OpenAI, etc.) via the app settings.

Can I export my data?

Currently, you can view all your entries in the app. Export functionality is planned for a future update.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. Micro Mood Journal is completely free to use, with all features available at no cost.

What AI models does it support?

We support OpenAI, Groq (free), Hugging Face, Cohere, Together AI, and a local fallback. You can choose your preferred model in settings.

How is the mood detected?

The app analyzes the text you write, identifying emotional keywords and sentiments to suggest a mood level from 1 (sad) to 5 (happy).
